Privacy Policy:


Almendra-Vilas, Turismo e Gestão real estate, SA, Legal Entity nº 505535181, with registered office in Quinta da Mesquita, Sítio da Mesquita, EN269, PO box 241G, 8365-202 Pêra, owner of the establishment “Quinta da Mesquita, Turismo Rural” values the privacy and protection of personal data collected from its customers, being responsible for their processing.

Whenever personal information from data subjects is necessary for the purposes of providing services, the use of that information is described in accordance with the terms of this document and with respect for data protection legislation (Regulation EU 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council ), applicable from May 25, 2018, to all EU Member States.


Registration and use of personal data


The collection and processing of customer identification data is intended for registration in the Quinta da Mesquita database, with the aim of carrying out the provision of hotel services, with identification data being processed via citizen card or passport, as well as address, email, telephone data, although other personal data that may be necessary or convenient for the provision of services may be collected, and tax identification data for legal and billing purposes.

The information collected is used for the room reservation and billing processes, customer management, legal obligations, for historical and statistical purposes, service quality control and for future contacts.

Data is collected through our website when the user sends us a reservation request or through our partner reservation platforms, in particular Booking.com.

We do not collect data from special categories of data and do not accept reservations made by children, therefore we do not collect personal information from children.

The personal data collected by us is not shared with third parties without the owner's consent, except for the exercise of legal obligations.

Personal data is kept for the period of time necessary for processing in accordance with current Portuguese legislation. Billing data is retained for a period of 10 years and customer identification data is retained for as long as necessary, normally 2 years.


Rights of holders


Data subjects have the right to access, rectify or update personal information stored about them, the right to portability, the right to delete their data and the right to limit or oppose the processing of their data.

Requests to exercise rights or other questions related to this topic can be made by letter addressed to “Quinta da Mesquita, Sítio da Mesquita, EN269, PO box 241G, 8365-202 Pera, or via email info@quintadamesquita.com.

Data security

 

Depending on the nature, scope, context and purposes of data processing, we undertake to apply, both when defining the means of processing and at the time of the processing itself, the technical and organizational measures necessary and appropriate to the protection of User Data and compliance with legal requirements.

We also undertake to ensure that, by default, only data that is necessary for each specific processing purpose is processed and that these data are not made available without human intervention to an indeterminate number of people.
